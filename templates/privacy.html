Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Your name and email address when you register.
  • A hashed version of your password — we never store it in plain text.
  • Expense records you create, including amount, category, date, and any description you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond running the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable precautions — including password hashing and access controls — to protect it from unauthorised access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we are committed to following best practices. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics services that collect your information. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated and you will be notified.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support page. We will respond as promptly as possible.
